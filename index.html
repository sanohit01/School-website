In charge of Atal Tinkering Lab



ATL Stands for ATAL Tinkering lab which is inaugurated with a special motive to push the students towards practical world and create something innovative which will bring the solution to the real world. This idea comes up with a motto of “making education reach everyone”.
It has various benefits like it will make the students realize their ideas through hands-on-it- yourself mode and also acquire innovation skills.
This initiative brings the curiosity, creativity and imagination in young minds with the help of which they can acquire the knowledge of STEM(Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) concepts.




I have already created SVMS pinjore Youtube channel




Features of ATL Lab-

  1. It contains educational and learning ‘do it yourself’ kits
  2. It contains equipment on science
  3. Basics are covered regarding electronics
  4. Concepts of robotics are implemented
  5. Open source microcontroller boards are available like Arduino and Node MCU
  6. Various sensors are used to get in touch with the real world examples
  7. 3D printers are used for creating 3 Dimensional objects
  8. Computers are installed for the purpose of Coding in embedded C language with the help of software called “Arduino IDE” and for creating 3D models on the online platform known as “ Tinkercad ”
